A diverse food scene awaits you


Four distinct seasons, fertile soils and mild temperatures means our region is rich in fruit, vegetables, herbs, lamb, beef, pork and chicken. This makes for a vibrant and varied array of eateries to choose from in Rotorua. From fine dining, artisan cafés and restaurants, to pub fare, three weekly markets, steambox (meals cooked using natural sources of geothermal steam), hāngī (a traditional Māori underground cooking method), locally brewed craft beer and wine – Rotorua has it all and the list continues to grow. With the bustling hub of Eat Streat and a steady stream of new and innovative cafés and restaurants opening their doors, Rotorua has become a well-known foodie destination with a global smorgasbord of gastronomical delights.

Eat Street


The bustling hub of Eat Streat boasts a dozen or so restaurants and bars, one boutique nightclub, and the iconic Lady Janes Ice Cream Parlour – all in one spot that’s as long as a city block. 
The covered central walkway is the focal point for this all-weather al fresco dining area, making it ideal for year-round entertainment.
 
Found at the “lake end” of Tutanekai Street, you’ll satisfy any craving from pub fare and steaks, to tapas, Indian, Italian and much more.

Capers Cafe


Capers is a contemporary café providing good old fashioned service for breakfast, lunch, dinner and takeout. For over a decade the friendly team at Capers have been making and serving good coffee and wholesome food for Rotorua locals and visitors.

If its a catch up with friends, business breakfasts, weekend brunch, food on the go, or a dinner date - Capers has it covered.



Skyline Stratosfare Restaurant and Bar

At Skyline Rotorua, our commitment to quality begins long before our produce arrives at our mountain-top site. We continuously search far and wide for the finest quality produce in New Zealand.

Feast on a delicious combination of our brasserie-style buffet, fresh seafood specialties, cook to order grill and mouth-watering desserts – all with the added bonus of Skyline’s unrivalled views across Lake Rotorua and surrounds.
