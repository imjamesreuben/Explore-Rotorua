Why leave just yet?



One thing Rotorua isn’t short on is a range of accommodation to suit any budget, taste and location.
 
If you’re staying for a few days, treat yourself to a suite at a five-star hotel or a charming room in a historic hotel built in 1897. Well-known brands such as the Holiday Inn and Accor hotels are here, as well as lovely boutique hotels, resorts and retreats. Most are conveniently located within the city, but several are a bit more off the beaten track.

Get comfortable and relax! 


Aura Accommodation

Aura Accommodation is Rotorua's latest piece of accommodation bliss. In the heart of where it's happening, only a short stroll from the lakefront, Government Gardens, and the famous 'Eat Streat'.


Unwind in our 2 private hot mineral pool, or get the steam box hissing for a geothermal cooking experience. Make yourself comfortable in any of our fully equipped units, whether a Studio, 1 Bedroom, 2 Bedroom or Family Unit, all complete with kitchen facilities and Sky TV, there is something there to suit every occasion. 

Let the friendly young local team show you true relaxed kiwi hospitality, connecting you to all the very best of what the region has on offer.  



Rotorua Thermal Holiday Park

Affordable holiday park accommodation close to Rotorua’s popular geothermal attractions & mountain biking trails.

We offer affordable accommodation options for singles, couples, families and groups. We have a large range of campsites for camping, campervanning & motorhomes as well as cabins, backpacker dorms and modern motel units.

Facilities include covered walkways to kitchen facilities, dining room, lounge, shower, toilets and laundry. We have on offer free heated swimming pools, hot mineral pools, games and TV rooms, barbecues and children's play area.

Cedarwood Lakeside - Motel & Conference Venue

Rotorua’s best kept secret! Quiet, tranquil lakefront accommodation and conference venue on the shores of Lake Rotorua, Self contained studio & family units, free WiFi, heated pool and spa in a relaxing scenic setting. Enjoy spectacular views of the lake and Mokoia Island and romantic evening sunsets.

The complex features private indoor spa pool, an outside spa pool, heated outdoor pool, large BBQ area, children's play area and private jetty with a nearby boat ramp. Coin operated guest laundry available. Plenty of off street parking for boats. Mountain bike wash down area and storage available. Breakfasts available - continental or cooked.
